About Our Congregation

Address: 1266 50th Street, Brooklyn, NY 11205

Welcome to Congregation Minchas Yehuda, a center for spiritual growth, community service, and Jewish education in Brooklyn. Our congregation is dedicated to fostering a warm and inclusive environment where individuals and families can connect with Jewish tradition and values.

We offer regular prayer services, Torah study classes, youth programs, and a variety of community events throughout the year. Our mission is to promote Jewish education, observance, and community spirit.

Services & Schedule

We offer a variety of religious services and educational programs throughout the week:

  • Daily Services: Morning (Shacharis), Afternoon (Mincha), and Evening (Maariv)
  • Shabbat Services: Friday evening and Saturday morning
  • Torah Study: Weekly classes for all ages and knowledge levels
  • Youth Programs: Educational activities for children and teenagers
  • Community Events: Holiday celebrations, social gatherings, and special events

Please contact us for the current schedule of services and programs.
